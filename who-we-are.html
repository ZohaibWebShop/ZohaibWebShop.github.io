Who We Serve in the Madison Valley

Madison Range supports property owners, STR hosts, guests, and locals with reliable, Montana-built solutions.

Absentee &
Seasonal Homeowners

Second homes, cabins, and ranch properties owned by people who can't be here year-round. You need local eyes and someone who understands Montana winters.

  • Scheduled home watch and photo reports
  • Storm and freeze checks after major weather
  • Arrival prep and seasonal transitions
  • Single point of contact for emergencies

Short-Term Rental
Owners & Managers

Airbnb/VRBO hosts and professional managers who need reliable local support to maintain guest satisfaction and protect rental income.

  • Between-guest turnovers and condition checks
  • On-call response for guest issues
  • Listing care and photo updates
  • Vendor coordination (cleaning/maintenance)

Local Ranches,
Trades & Businesses

Ranches, contractors, and operators who need welding, field repair, storage, and project support in the Ennis area.

  • Field welding and equipment repair
  • Storage for vehicles, trailers, and gear
  • Trade coordination and project oversight
  • Local accountability for specialized tasks

One Call for Everything

For our clients, we are the only call they make when anything comes up at their home or rental – we handle it or coordinate the right local pro and stay accountable end-to-end.
